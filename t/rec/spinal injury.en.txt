<center><b>Spine injuries</b>
Spinal injury is a serious type of injury. Dislocations and fractures of the thoracic and lumbar vertebrae are accompanied by pain in the area of the damaged vertebra. If the spinal cord is damaged, there may be impaired sensitivity and movement in the limbs (paralysis).</center>

<b>Moving or shifting the victim</b>
It should be carried out with the help of several people, special attention should be paid to fixing the cervical spine.
